70:## (2) Failure-Handling Semantics
71:
72:- Lint steps that specify `continue-on-error: true` SHALL continue execution
73:  while still recording failure status for downstream guards.
74:- A fail guard such as `if: failure()` SHALL be present to ensure the workflow
75:  reacts deterministically to prior lint failures.
76:- Steps using `if: always()` for log generation and artifact upload SHALL
77:  execute regardless of previous outcomes to guarantee evidence capture.
78:- Always-run steps SHALL only be employed when upstream steps cannot abort the
79:  job; otherwise the workflow risks silent termination.
